# KORRA: Global Distance & Individual User Refactor Plan
**Focus**: Individual Users + Global Tailoring Infrastructure
**Goal**: Highlight the "Distance Factor" as a critical pain point for both users (Lagos/London/Global) and investors (DIV Fund).

---

## 1. The "Distance Factor" Pain Point
*   **The Problem**: Getting measured is currently a physical constraint. If your tailor is in another city or country (e.g., London client, Lagos tailor), you are forced to re-measure manually, leading to error and waste.
*   **The KORRA Fix**: "Distance-Free Precision." Accurate data captured in London is identical to what the tailor receives in Lagos.

## 2. Capturing the Individual User
*   **Narrative**: "Own Your Biometric Identity."
*   **Hook**: Stop re-measuring for every new outfit or every new tailor. Scan once, share globally.

---

## 3. High-Impact Content Expansion

### A. New Section: "Bridging the Distance Gap" (Post-Paradox)
*   **Visual**: A map-style graphic showing data traveling from User (Location A) to Tailor (Location B).
*   **Copy**: "Distance is no longer a barrier to the perfect fit."
*   **Outcome**: High-fidelity measurements shared across borders instantly. No flights, no manual tapes, no guesswork.

### B. Refactored "Persistence" -> "Global Size Passport"
*   **Old**: "Lifetime Size Record."
*   **New**: "Your Global Size Passport."
*   **Copy**: One digital twin for all your bespoke needs. Use it for your local tailor, your international artisan, or your favorite online boutique.

### C. DIV Fund Alignment: "Borderless Economic Impact"
*   **Narrative**: Reducing the friction of international artisan commerce. Scaling the reach of craftsmen in emerging markets to global clients.
