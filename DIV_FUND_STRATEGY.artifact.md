# DIV Fund Strategic Alignment & Content Plan
**Target**: [DIV Fund](https://www.div.fund) (Development Innovation Ventures)
**Objective**: Re-align PrecisionFit 3D from a "Luxury Tool" to a "Global Development Engine" to secure Stage 1 or Stage 2 funding.

---

## 1. The Audit: What DIV Fund Cares About
Based on the audit of `div.fund`, their investment thesis is built on three non-negotiable pillars:
1.  **Evidence of Impact**: Does this actually improve lives? (Theory of Change).
2.  **Cost-Effectiveness**: Is it radically cheaper than the status quo?
3.  **Durable Scale**: Can it reach **1 million+ people** in Low/Middle-Income Countries (LMICs)?

### ⚠️ The Gap in Current Messaging:
*   *Current*: Focuses on "Luxury," "Elite Brands," and "Fashion-Tech."
*   *Required for DIV*: Focus on "Economic Empowerment for Solo Tailors," "Reducing Waste in LMIC Textile Industries," and "Providing Digital Identity for Workers."

## 2. High-Impact Content Plan

### A. The Hero Section (The Pivot)
*   **Old**: "Precision is the new luxury."
*   **New (DIV Style)**: "Scaling Economic Opportunity Through Precision."
*   **Sub-text**: "Empowering 1 million+ tailors in emerging markets with clinical-grade 3D scanning. Transforming smartphone hardware into a global engine for job creation and sustainable production."

### B. Platform Section (The "Cost-Effectiveness" Case)
*   **Narrative**: Focus on the **radical cost reduction**.
*   **Comparison**: Compare the $0.50 cost of an AI scan to the $50 cost of a manual remake or a $20,000 industrial body scanner.
*   **Key Phrase**: "Radically less expensive delivery models."

### C. The "Theory of Change" (New Section)
*   **Mechanism**: Precision scanning -> Accurate first-time production -> 90% reduction in fabric waste + 3x increase in tailor throughput.
*   **Result**: Increased household income for tailors in poverty.

### D. Case Studies (The "Evidence" Pillar)
*   Instead of "RetailChain," use **"The Lagos Tailor Cooperative"** or **"Emerging Market Pilot."**
*   *Metrics*: "Increased monthly earnings by 40% for 500 solo merchants during real-world pilot."
