Our Roadmap

The path to 98% clinical accuracy.

Our development is guided by rigorous testing and transparency, designed to meet the high standards of evidence-based funds like DIV.fund.

1
Foundation & Data Capture

We built the infrastructure to securely store and analyze high-resolution body data. This baseline allows us to track accuracy improvements across every demographic and body archetype.

2
The Human Feedback Loop

Approved Master Tailors act as "Algorithm Contributors." Their real-world expertise is the primary source of truth that helps us eliminate systematic biases in traditional AI models.

3
Iterative Shape Fitting

Moving beyond one-shot predictions, our system now uses real-time optimization to "fit" the 3D model to your actual photo silhouette, drastically reducing measurement error.

Universal Fit Lockdown

Reaching a global gold standard of < 1cm average error. This level of precision enables fully automated, zero-fitting manufacturing for the global artisan economy.
