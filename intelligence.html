The 4 Pillars of Precision

How we hit 98% accuracy.

PILLAR 01

Data Flywheel

We capture raw SMPL parameters on every scan. When master tailors edit a result, our AI "back-calculates" the 3D shape to learn the truth from the human expert.

PILLAR 02

Anatomical Prior (GMM)

Using Gaussian Mixture Models, we define the "probability of human-ness." If the AI predicts an impossible shape, our prior "shrinks" it back to a biologically realistic state.

PILLAR 03

Silhouette Optimization

Unlike 1-shot models, KORRA iterates. We project the 3D mesh to 2D and refine the shape coefficients until the projection perfectly overlaps the user's actual silhouette.

PILLAR 04

Subgroup Calibration

We don't use generic gender averages. Our system automatically clusters users into "Body Archetypes" (Athletic, Curvy, etc.) to apply specialized calibration factors.
