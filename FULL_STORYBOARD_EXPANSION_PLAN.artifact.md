## 1. The New Storyboard Architecture

### Section 1: The Status Quo Crisis ("Manual measuring slows everything down")
*   **Visual**: A chaotic split-screen of manual tape measures vs. the stable KORRA grid.
*   **Copy**: Focus on the **Mistakes, Extra Fittings, and Disputes**.
*   **Pain Points**: "Fit still goes wrong," "Delayed Delivery," "Disputes over error."

### Section 2: The Standardization Gap ("Sizing has no standard")
*   **Visual**: Data visualization showing the **62% consumer struggle**.
*   **Copy**: Explain that custom tailoring fails when measurements are collected inconsistently across different users.
*   **KORRA Edge**: Clinical consistency across all users and locations.

### Section 3: Precision at Source ("A faster way to measure")
*   **Visual**: High-detail "BODY CAPTURE" technical image.
*   **Copy**: 3 steps (Scan, Measure, Use). 60 seconds from scan to result.

### Section 4: The Lifetime Record ("Know your measurements when you need them")
*   **Visual**: A digital "Size Passport" UI mockup.
*   **Copy**: No more guessing. Keep one simple size record for tailoring and online shopping.

### Section 5: The Economic Engine ("Less time. Less waste.")
*   **Visual**: Comparison chart (Old Remake Loss vs. KORRA Profitability).
*   **Copy**: Lower labor time, zero rework, more customers served per worker.
