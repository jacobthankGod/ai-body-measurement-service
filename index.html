The Problem

Remote apparel still runs on guesswork.

When measurements are shared through chats, photos, voice notes, or old records, fit becomes inconsistent. That leads to remakes, delays, extra fittings, and unhappy customers.

For tailors, it means more wasted time. For customers, it means poor fit and frustration. For businesses, it means a broken process that does not scale.

The Frustration Loop

The cost of bad measurements adds up fast.

A cycle of failed expectations created by inconsistent data.

Global Reach

Built for distance, designed for trust.

KORRA helps tailors and apparel businesses serve customers beyond their immediate location.

That matters most in markets where remote orders, diaspora buyers, and cross-border tailoring are already part of how business works.

Korra makes remote tailoring more reliable by helping customers capture and share accurate body measurements directly from their phone. This gives tailors a more consistent way to work with remote clients, reduce rework, improve fit confidence, and serve more customers across cities and borders.
