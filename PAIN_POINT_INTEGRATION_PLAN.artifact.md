# Pain Point Integration Plan (KORRA v1.1)
**Goal**: Explicitly address the "Frustration Loop" of custom tailoring to increase conversion and DIV Fund impact.

---

## 1. The Missing Pain Points Audit

### A. The "Dispute" Problem (Fit Still Goes Wrong)
*   **Narrative**: Even with manual measurements, garments are often too tight or too small. This leads to toxic disputes between tailors and customers about "who measured wrong."
*   **The KORRA Fix**: "Clinical Evidence." The scan is an objective source of truth.

### B. The "Timeline" Problem (Delayed Delivery)
*   **Narrative**: Scheduled trial fittings are a nightmare. Multiple revision rounds cause deliveries to exceed agreed dates.
*   **The KORRA Fix**: "First-Time Perfection." Eliminate the need for trial fittings.

### C. The "Standardization" Crisis (Sizing has no standard)
*   **Data Point**: 62% of consumers struggle with fit.
*   **Narrative**: Even custom tailoring fails when measurements are collected inconsistently across different users.
*   **The KORRA Fix**: "Unified Biometric Identity." One standard for all clients.
