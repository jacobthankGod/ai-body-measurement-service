Measurement Guide

Learn how to take optimal photos for accurate AI body measurements. Follow these guidelines to achieve ±1-3cm accuracy.

📸 Photo Requirements

✅

Camera Specifications

  • Minimum resolution: 256x256 pixels
  • Recommended: 1080x1920 or higher (portrait)
  • Any modern smartphone camera works
  • Front-facing or rear camera acceptable
📐

Framing Guidelines

  • Full body visible from head to toes
  • Stand 2-3 meters (6-10 feet) from camera
  • Arms slightly away from body (~15cm)
  • Feet shoulder-width apart
💡

Lighting Requirements

  • Avoid harsh direct sunlight
  • Use indirect natural light when possible
  • Even lighting on front and side of body
  • Avoid strong shadows on body
👕

Clothing Recommendations

  • Tight-fitting clothing shows body outline
  • Avoid loose/bulky garments
  • Form-fitting t-shirt and leggings ideal
  • Remove accessories that affect outline

📋 Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Prepare the Space

  • Find a well-lit area with plain background
  • Ensure 2-3 meters (6-10 feet) of clear space
  • Check camera screen shows full body

2. Take Front Photo

  • Stand straight facing camera
  • Feet shoulder-width apart
  • Arms slightly away from sides
  • Look straight ahead
  • Take photo at eye level or slightly above

3. Take Side Photo

  • Turn 90 degrees from camera
  • Ensure full profile is visible
  • Arms still slightly away from body
  • Take photo at same height as front

4. Review & Upload

  • Check both photos show full body clearly
  • Ensure body outline is not obscured
  • Upload both to API endpoint

⚠️ Common Issues to Avoid

Issue Problem Solution
Body cut off Head or feet not visible Step back further from camera
Too close Body fills entire frame Move 2-3 meters away
Bad lighting Too dark or too bright Find even indirect light
Loose clothing Cannot see body outline Wear form-fitting clothes
Blurry photo Camera moved Hold steady or use timer
