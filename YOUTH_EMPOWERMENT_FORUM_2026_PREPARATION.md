## PART 1: PROJECT OVERVIEW FOR YOUR APPLICATION

### What is KORRA?

KORRA is an AI-powered body measurement platform that uses smartphone cameras to capture clinical-grade body measurements in 60 seconds. Users scan themselves, get a precise 3D model with 85+ body measurements, and can share their "digital size profile" with any tailor or brand worldwide.

### The Problem We Solve

- **Remote tailoring fails** because measurements shared via chat/voice/notes often result in poor fit
- This leads to costly remakes (30%+ of orders), delayed delivery, and customer disappointment
- The global fashion market ($1.5T) loses billions annually to measurement errors
- Tailors in developing countries (LMICs) cannot serve international customers due to the "fittings barrier"

### Our Solution

Using AI and computer vision to turn a smartphone into a 3D body scanner - no expensive hardware required.

**Key Metrics:**
- 90% reduction in fabric waste from measurement error
- 2x more customers served per worker daily
- $0.50 cost per measurement scan
- 85+ body measurements extracted from 2 photos
- Works globally - anyone with a smartphone can use it

### Quick Pitch (30 seconds)

> "KORRA is an AI body measurement platform. Think of it like a 'fingerprint for fit'—you scan yourself once with your phone, and we generate 85+ precise body measurements. These can be shared with any tailor anywhere in the world. It's solving the $100B+ problem of poor fit in custom clothing."

### Problem-Solution Format

**Problem**: "Remote custom tailoring doesn't work because accurate measurements can't be captured remotely. This costs the fashion industry billions annually in returns and remakes."

**Solution**: "We use AI to turn any smartphone into a clinical-grade body scanner. Two photos. 60 seconds. 85+ measurements."

**Impact**: "We're helping tailors in developing countries serve global customers, reducing fabric waste by 90%."

### Technical Depth (if asked)

- **Tech Stack**: Python, FastAPI, MediaPipe, Supabase (Auth + Database), JavaScript frontend
- **AI Method**: Pose estimation using Google's MediaPipe to identify body landmarks, then geometric calculations for measurements
- **Architecture**: RESTful API with API key authentication
- **Deployment**: Vercel serverless

### Challenges & How You Overcame Them

Possible challenges to discuss:
- Getting consistent measurement accuracy
- Handling different body types
- Mobile browser compatibility
- Privacy/security of biometric data
- Making the technology accessible in low-bandwidth areas
