Frequently asked questions

What is a scan credit?

A scan credit is one unit of measurement. One credit = one full body scan (front and side photos), generating 80+ body measurements and a 3D model.

Do credits expire?

No. Credits never expire. Use them at your own pace — whether that is one scan per week or a hundred in a day.

Can I get a refund?

Yes. If you have not used any credits, we offer a full refund within 14 days of purchase. Used credits are non-refundable.

How do volume discounts work?

Discounts apply automatically when you cross a threshold. For custom quantities above 150 credits, the discounted rate is calculated in real time.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cards, bank transfers, and mobile money. All transactions are processed securely via our payment gateway.
