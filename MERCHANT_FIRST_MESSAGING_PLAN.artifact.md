# Merchant-First Messaging Plan (Tailor Focus)
**Goal**: Speak directly to solo tailors and boutique shop owners while keeping the high-impact "Expert" feel.

## 1. Copy Audit & Refinement

### A. The Hero Section
*   **Old**: "Scaling Opportunity Through Precision" (Too academic)
*   **New**: "Master Your Patterns. Scale Your Shop."
*   **Sub-text**: "The smartphone-only tool for solo tailors. Get clinical-grade 3D measurements in seconds, eliminate remakes, and grow your income with untouchable accuracy."

### B. The Core Sections
*   **Technology**: Focus on **"Perfect First-Time Fits"** instead of "Delivery Models."
*   **Case Studies**: Change "Lagos Pilot" to **"The Success of Solo Tailors."** Focus on how much money they saved and how many clients they gained.
*   **Benefits**: Use verbs like **"Deliver," "Grow," "Build"** instead of "Evaluation" or "Mechanism."

### C. The Final CTA
*   **Context**: The "Pilot Proposal" language is nonsense for a tailor.
*   **The Fix**: "Ready to Double Your Output?"
*   **Action**: "Start Your 7-Day Precision Trial." (Simple, actionable, merchant-focused).

---

## 2. Structural Integrity
*   **Container**: Keep the 1150px "Nops" width.
*   **Imagery**: Keep the 1.5fr "Large Picture" grid.
*   **Typography**: Keep the Bold 900 weight for authority.

**Protocol**: Every change pushed to GitHub immediately.
