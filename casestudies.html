Desby: The Fashion Operating System.

KORRA partnered with Desby to transform traditional tailoring into a high-tech operation. By using KORRA's accurate 3D body models directly inside the Desby studio, artisans can skip the struggle of manual measurements. This partnership allows tailors to create perfect patterns automatically, reducing wait times by 60% and ensuring every garment fits the customer perfectly.
