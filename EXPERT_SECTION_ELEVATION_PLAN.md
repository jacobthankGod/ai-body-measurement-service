# Expert Section Elevation Plan (Premium Scaling)

This plan "increases" the impact of the core marketing sections to achieve an expert, high-fidelity SaaS aesthetic while respecting the compact 1000px width.

## 1. Visual Weight Enhancement
The goal is to make the sections feel more commanding and "Expensive."

### Step 1: Premium Breathability
*   **Vertical Padding**: Increase `section.block` padding from 100px to **160px**. 
*   **Reasoning**: High-end SaaS brands (Linear, Stripe) use vertical white space to signal confidence and luxury.

### Step 2: Commanding Typography
*   **Section Titles**: Scale up from 36px to **56px** with a tighter line-height (1.0).
*   **Descriptions**: Scale up from 16px to **20px** for easier readability and authority.
*   **Eyebrows**: Add a subtle `text-shadow: 0 0 10px var(--Mint)` to the electric mint eyebrows to lead the user's eye.

### Step 3: Card & Media Impact
*   **Glass Cards**: Increase inner padding from 28px to **40px**. 
*   **Images**: Scale the "Body Capture Visualization" and "3D Zones" images to be more prominent within the 1000px container.
*   **Radius**: Increase `border-radius` of cards to **24px** for a more modern, friendly-yet-precise feel.

## 2. Component-Specific Polish
*   **Steps 01-04**: Make the numbers (01, 02...) much larger and semi-transparent behind the text for a "Gallery" feel.
*   **Wellness Widget**: Increase the height of the "Target Track" and add a subtle pulse to the "LIVE" indicator.
