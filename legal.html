Data Privacy & Terms

1. Data Autonomy

We believe biometric identity should belong to the individual. All photo data captured via KORRA is processed using edge-AI encryption. Original images are never stored on our permanent servers once the 3D landmark extraction is complete.

2. Artisan Integrity

Merchants utilizing the KORRA API agree to use biometric data solely for the purpose of garment production and fit recommendation. Commercial resale of individual measurement data is strictly prohibited under our Global Artisan Compact.

3. Accuracy Guarantee

KORRA provides measurements with a clinical average variance of ±1cm. While our AI engine is highly precise, final garment fit depends on pattern-making execution. KORRA provides the data; the artisan provides the artistry.

4. Subscription Terms

Scan credits are billed at a rate of $0.50 per successful extraction. Credits remain valid in your Biometrics Vault for 12 months from the date of purchase.
