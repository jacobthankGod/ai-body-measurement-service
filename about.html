KORRA wasn't born to replace the tape measure. It was born to eliminate the distance between the world's most talented artisans and the clients who value their craft, but live thousands of miles away.

1. The Problem: The fittings barrier
Global artisan supply chains are currently blocked by a "fittings barrier." Tailors in developing countries are often limited to their local area because custom production requires accurate body data that is traditionally impossible to capture remotely. Self-reported measurements are the primary cause of fabric waste and poor customer fit.


2. The Solution: Smartphone AI
KORRA makes accurate measurements accessible by using the power of your smartphone. By removing the need for expensive laser scanners, we create a low-cost system that empowers tailors everywhere to compete on a global stage.
