Our Mission

Scaling Global Artisan Infrastructure.

KORRA was built to solve the "Distance Factor"—the fundamental gap between elite craftsmanship and global clients. For too long, the physical requirement of a fitting has limited the growth of independent tailors and artisans in emerging economies.

By offloading clinical-grade 3D body modeling to the smartphone, we remove the need for expensive industrial hardware and physical travel. We empower a tailor in Lagos to serve a client in London with absolute fit confidence, ensuring that quality artistry is never restricted by location.

Our goal is to build a borderless digital ecosystem where biometric identity is portable, precision is automated, and fashion waste is a relic of the past.

Precision First

±1cm accuracy through advanced computer vision, ensuring the first cut is the final cut.

Economic Inclusion

Hardware-free infrastructure designed to reach the next 1 billion users in LMICs.

Sustainability

90% reduction in fabric waste by eliminating measurement error and multiple remakes.
